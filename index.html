What Is Golden Jewels Junior School ?

Golden Jewels Junior School is a Christian school dedicated to academic excellence, character development and holistic education. We inspire children to become confident, responsible and compassionate leaders.

Vision

To create an environment where each child's God given ability is identified and nurtured.

Mission

To provide quality Christian education that nurtures learners spiritually, academically, socially and emotionally.
