Who We Are

Golden Jewels Junior School is committed to providing quality Christian education that develops every learner academically, spiritually, socially and emotionally. Our caring teachers work closely with parents to ensure each child reaches his or her full potential.

Vision

To create an environment where each child's God-given ability is identified and nurtured.

Mission

To provide quality Christian education that inspires excellence, character and leadership.
